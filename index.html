Common questions

What exactly do I get when I buy a bundle?

A ZIP containing each skill's SKILL.md and supporting files (scripts, references, configs). Unzip into ~/.hermes/skills/ and your agent loads them on the next turn. You get a private download link valid for 5 downloads.

How are the skills licensed?

MIT-style for personal and commercial use. You can modify, redistribute inside your company, and ship derivatives. You can't resell the original ZIP.

What about refunds?

14-day no-questions refund if the bundle doesn't ship value for you. Email refunds@skillhub.dev with your order ID. Chargebacks processed via Lemonsqueezy dashboard in <48h.

Do I get updates when new skills ship?

Yes — lifetime free updates for any bundle you buy. When we add a new skill to a bundle, you get an email with a fresh download link.

Why not just use the free skills already in the repo?

You can — these are the same skills, packaged. Buying a bundle funds continued curation, the bundle packaging, the documentation polish, and the payment infrastructure. Think of it as "support the curator" + skip the assembly work.

Can I submit my own skill?

Yes. Use the GitHub repo's skill submission template. Approved skills get a 70/30 revenue split via Stripe Connect once we hit month 3.
